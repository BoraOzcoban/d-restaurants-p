Randomrestaurants



The site aims to show the user a random restaurant/ cafe in a random European city or the city of choice. Users can use these functions in a variety of ways such as saving time by letting us show them a place to eat in their city or selecting a random restaurant in a random city in Europe to go for an adventurous vacation... Also, the user can see where they can find their favorite cuisine the most.



At the moment, the cities available are: Amsterdam (Netherlands), Athens (Greece), Barcelona (Spain), Berlin (Germany), Bratislava (Slovakia), Bruxelles (Bruxelles), Budapest (Hungary), Copenhagen (Denmark), Dublin (Ireland), Edinburgh (United Kingdom), Geneva (Switzerland), Helsinki (Finland), Hamburg (Germany), Krakow (Poland), Lisbon (Portugal), Ljubljana (Slovenia), London (United Kingdom), Luxembourg (Luxembourg), Madrid (Spain), Lyon (France), Milan (Italy), Munich (Germany), Oporto (Portgual), Oslo (Norway), Paris (France), Prague (Czech Republic), Rome (Italy), Stockholm (Sweden), Vienna (Austria), Warsaw (Poland), Zurich (Switzerland)
