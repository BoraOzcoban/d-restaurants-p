Country of the Month:

Turkey

Turkey is a beautiful country with its magnificent natural beauties such as the Cappadocia, the Aegean coast, green forests of the Black Sea. And its wonderful historical buildings such as the Hagia Sophia, the Blue Mosque, Topkapı Palace. If you come to Turkey, you will not only get fascinated by the beautiful touristic places, you will also enjoy the delicious Turkish cuisine. You will enjoy eating the most known Turkish dishes such as kebab and döner in their hometown and believe me, you will most certainly love them. But Turkish cuisine is not limited to kebab and döner, here is a list of some awesome Turkish dishes that I have chosen from hundreds of various dishes:

Pide: A doughy meat pie but it also has vegetarian options such as Pide with Cheese. And my favorite type of pide is the pide with grounded meat and eggs. It tastes even better with Ayran.

Cag Kebab: A special type of kebab from the city of Erzurum, a mountainous city perfect for winter tourism, which is made with Goat or Lamb.

Simit: As known as Turkish Bagels in the United States and Gevrek in Izmir, it is a delicious circular bread with sesame seeds.

Cacık: It is salted strained yoghurt with cucumbers, garlic, olive oil and a lot more.

Lahmacun: Also known as the Turkish Pizza, it is also a pretty good one to try.

Manti: Turkish dumpling which gets awesome with its special sauce.

Dolma: Peppers stuffed with rice and meat(not required).

Boyoz: A Turkish pastry that you can find in İzmir

Turkish cuisine also has a great variety of delicious sweets and desserts you should try such as:

Turkish Delight: a small sweet most people eat alongside Turkish Coffee. It has a lot of options that are less or more intense.

Baklava: A famous layered pastry dessert filled with either pistachio or nuts

Kazandibi: A caramelized milk pudding you should try.

Künefe: It is a dessert made with shredded filo pastry.

Sutlac: Baked Rice Pudding containing milk

Turkish cuisine also contains awesome drinks you should try such as:

Ayran: The perfect cold and healthy drink for hot summer days. It is made of yoghurt, salt and water.

Şalgam: Traditional turnip juice.
