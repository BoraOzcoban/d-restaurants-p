Randomrestaurants



Don't want to lose time looking for a restaurant? Want to try something new? Just click one of the random restaurant selector tools and enjoy











At the moment, cities available are: Amsterdam (Netherlands), Athens (Greece) , Barcelona (Spain) , Berlin (Germany), Bratislava (Slovakia), Bruxelles (Bruxelles), Budapest (Hungary), Copenhagen (Denmark), Dublin (Ireland), Edinburgh (United Kingdom), Geneva (Switzerland), Helsinki (Finland), Hamburg (Germany), Krakow (Poland), Lisbon (Portugal), Ljubljana (Slovenia), London (United Kingdom), Luxembourg (Luxembourg), Madrid (Spain), Lyon (France), Milan (Italy), Munich (Germany), Oporto (Portgual), Oslo (Norway), Paris (France), Prague (Czech Republic), Rome (Italy), Stockholm (Sweden), Vienna (Austria), Warsaw (Poland), Zurich (Switzerland)



The data I used for this project is from TripAdvisor Restaurants Info for 31 Euro-Cities dataset by Damien BENESCHI, so it may not contain all the restaurants in the city. Thus, the rankings provided in the last buttons may not be completely accurate. Yet, you can get an idea about whether there are a lot of restaurants offering your favorite cuisine in a city or not.
